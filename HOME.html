DID YOU KNOW

Baguio, often called the "Summer Capital of the Philippines," is nestled in the Cordillera Central mountains. Known for its cool climate, lush pine trees, and panoramic vistas, it's a haven for both locals seeking respite from the tropical heat and tourists eager to explore its vibrant markets, cultural sites, and outdoor adventures. From the bustling Session Road to the serene Burnham Park and the majestic Mines View Park, Baguio offers a blend of urban charm and natural beauty that captivates visitors all year round.
