What is the Session Road?

Session Road is a famous street in Baguio City, Philippines. It's one of the main thoroughfares in the city and is known for its vibrant atmosphere, lined with shops, restaurants, bars, and entertainment venues. Session Road is often bustling with activity, especially during peak tourist seasons. It's a popular destination for both locals and visitors alike, offering a taste of Baguio's culture, cuisine, and nightlife.

HISTORY BACKGROUND

Session Road

In the early days of the city's development. It was named after the Baguio Legislative Council's "session" meetings that were held there during the American colonial period. Originally a dirt trail, Session Road gradually evolved into a bustling commercial center as Baguio City grew in importance as a hill station and summer capital during the early 20th century. Over the years, it became the main thoroughfare of the city, lined with shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues, catering to both locals and tourists. Session Road has witnessed significant events in Baguio's history and has remained a central hub for commerce, culture, and social life in the city.
